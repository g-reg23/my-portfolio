      What is SVG cut files?
      What is an SVG Cut File? SVG stands for Scalable Vector Graphic. It is a two-dimensional XML-based vector image format. In plain English: It's a vector file that will tell your cutting machine where to cut lines and you can scale it (make it larger or smaller) without ruining its structure!
